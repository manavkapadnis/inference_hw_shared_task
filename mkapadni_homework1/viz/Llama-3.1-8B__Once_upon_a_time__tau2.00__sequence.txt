She had a twin brother who was also born with Down syndrome. She had a brother who was born just three years later who also had Down syndrome. Her parents never considered terminating either pregnancy when they learned their unborn child might have a disability. They did not see Down syndrome as a reason to terminate a life. To them, their children were gifts.
